OUR PROJECT ON SKIN DISEASES

Skin disease diagnosis is a critical aspect of healthcare, with skin conditions affecting millions of individuals worldwide. The proposed system - Skin Disease Image Processing System, aims to revolutionize the diagnosis and management of skin diseases through automated image analysis techniques. The input to the system is images of the affected skin areas, captured using a smartphone or digital camera. The system begins by segmenting the skin lesions from the surrounding healthy skin, using image processing algorithms. Once segmented, various features such as color, texture, and shape are extracted from the skin lesions. These features are then combined to form a feature vector, which represents the unique characteristics of each skin lesion.
